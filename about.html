Frequently Asked Questions

Is Senja Coffee halal

Yes, Senja Coffee is 100% halal, ensuring you can enjoy our beverages with complete peace of mind. We take pride in offering only the finest ingredients, all sourced and prepared in accordance with halal standards.

Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes! We offer a variety of plant-based milk options like oat, almond, and soy, so everyone can enjoy our delicious drinks, no matter their dietary preferences.

Can I order Senja Coffee online?

Yes, we offer convenient online ordering for both delivery and pickup. Visit our website or use our app to place your order and enjoy your coffee at home or in the office.

Is Senja Coffee fictitious?

Yes, Senja Coffee is just a fictitious website that aims to be the final project of the developer's education. Which means, all the contents of the website, both in the form of products and addresses, are fictional.
